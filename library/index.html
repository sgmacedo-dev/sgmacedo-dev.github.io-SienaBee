FROM THE LIBRARIAN

The Siena Bee Library is intentionally modest in number and ambitious in purpose. It does not aspire to gather every noteworthy publication, but rather to preserve those rare volumes that continue to illuminate the human condition across generations.

Each recommendation has been selected not merely for its intellectual merit, but for its enduring capacity to cultivate discernment, imagination and moral character. Books worthy of this House are not consumed. They are revisited.
