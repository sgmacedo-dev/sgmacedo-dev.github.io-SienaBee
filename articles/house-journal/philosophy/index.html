FROM THE EDITOR

The Philosophical Tradition of the House

Philosophy has never been merely an academic discipline. It is, above all, an education of perception. The essays gathered here seek not only to examine ideas, but to cultivate judgment, moderation and intellectual humility.

From the Stoics to contemporary reflections, each publication invites the reader into an unhurried conversation about the enduring questions of human existence.
