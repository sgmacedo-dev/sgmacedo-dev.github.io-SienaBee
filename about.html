About Siena Bee

A sanctuary for curious minds.

Welcome. My name is Silvana Macêdo, creator of Siena Bee.

I am fascinated by philosophy, neuroscience, scientific communication, books, photography and the deliberate practice of slow living.

Siena Bee was born from a simple idea: to cultivate silence in a world increasingly dominated by noise.

Here you'll find essays, reading notes, photography, carefully selected recommendations and reflections inspired by a quieter, more attentive way of living.

If these themes resonate with you, I hope this space feels less like a website and more like a library where every visit offers something worth taking with you.
